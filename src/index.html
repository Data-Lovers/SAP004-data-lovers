          ABOUT

          Rick and Morty is a series of American adult animated comedy and science fiction created
          Filmography by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon for the Adult Swim evening block, shown on channel
          Cartoon Network. The series debuted on December 2, 2013 and follows the scientist's dangerous
          adventures alcoholic Rick and his grandson Morty, who divides his time between family life and
          interdimensional travel. The series originated from an animated parody in the short film Back to
          the Future created by Roiland for Channel 101 film festival. Adult Swim approached Harmon about
          ideas for a television program, so he and Roiland developed the program based on the short, replacing
          the Doc characters with Rick and Marty with Morty. The show revolves around the adventures of family
          members Smith, which consists of parents Jerry and Beth, their children Summer and Morty, and Beth's
          father, named Rick Sanchez, who lives with them as a guest.
